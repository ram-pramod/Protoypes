Snapshots

Capture your server state before making changes. Up to 2 snapshots per VPS, free with your plan.

How snapshots work: A snapshot captures your full server state at the block level (crash-consistent, no downtime). Snapshots don't count toward your disk quota and never expire. Restoring overwrites your entire VPS — all data created after the snapshot will be lost.
